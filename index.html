My Background / About Me
I am Iñigo Lens Blasco, a computer engineer with special interest in video games, virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR), with a degree at the University of Deusto and currently studying the Master in Computer Graphics, Virtual Reality and Simulation at U-tad. My academic career and professional experience focus on the development of immersive applications and advanced simulations. During my studies, I have worked on projects such as OnionRing, an upcoming VR video game in collaboration with Gate8Games, and I have participated in various international GameJams and hackathons.
