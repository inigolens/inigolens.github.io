My Background / About Me
I am Iñigo Lens Blasco, a computer engineer specialized in video games, virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR), with training at the University of Deusto and currently studying the Master in Computer Graphics, Virtual Reality and Simulation at U-tad. My academic career and professional experience focus on the development of immersive applications and advanced simulations. During my studies and internships, I have worked on projects such as OnionRing, an upcoming VR video game in collaboration with Gate8Games, and I have participated in various international GameJams and hackathons that fostered my skills in agile development and creative problem solving.
